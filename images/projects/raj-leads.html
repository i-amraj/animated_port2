Project Overview

Raj Leads CRM is a comprehensive customer relationship management system designed to help businesses manage their leads, track sales pipelines, and automate marketing workflows. The platform provides real-time analytics, team collaboration tools, and seamless integration with popular business applications to streamline your sales process.

Key Features

Lead Management

Capture, qualify, and manage leads with customizable pipelines and automated scoring.

Sales Pipeline

Visual sales pipeline with drag-and-drop functionality to track deals through stages.

Email Integration

Send and track emails directly from the CRM with templates and automation.

Analytics Dashboard

Real-time analytics and reports to track performance and identify trends.

Task Automation

Automate repetitive tasks with workflows, reminders, and follow-up sequences.

Team Collaboration

Collaborate with team members, assign leads, and track team performance.
